About Tangub City

Tangub, officially the City of Tangub, is a component city in the province of Misamis Occidental, Philippines. According to the 2020 census, it has a population of 68,389 people. Tangub City is renowned as the "Christmas Symbols Capital of the Philippines" and is famous for its spectacular Christmas Festival of Lights.

The city is strategically located where the Panguil Bay Bridge connects Mindanao's regions, making it a vital transportation hub. Tangub City is blessed with natural wonders, including pristine beaches, lush mountains, and rich cultural heritage that attracts visitors from around the world.
